What is Zumbastico?

Zumbastico is an animation studio of content creators for kids & family. We have produced eight original series, including Paper Port (Gloob, Señal Colombia, Pakapaka, TVN), The Papernauts (Discovery Kids), Zumbastico Fantastico (Cartoon Network), and Horatio & The Plasticines (Pakapaka). The studio is currently in production of a new animation series, and development of two feature films. Since 2019 we are part of the Pipeline Studios Group (Canadá).
